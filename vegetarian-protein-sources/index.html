Vegetarian Protein Sources

Plant-based diets can provide all essential amino acids when properly planned. Lentils and beans offer protein plus valuable fiber for digestion. Quinoa is a complete protein containing all nine essential amino acids. Tofu and tempeh are versatile soy-based meat alternatives. Nuts and nut butters deliver protein along with healthy fats. Ancient grains like amaranth provide protein uncommon in Western diets. Combining rice with beans creates a complete protein profile.
